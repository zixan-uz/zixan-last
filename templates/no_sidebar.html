    • Recruitment and Employment Partnership

    At Zixan, we are committed to helping companies find and hire the most suitable candidates for their workforce needs. We offer a full-cycle recruitment service that is 100% FREE OF CHARGE for employers.

    We primarily focus on preparing and selecting professionals based on the specific demands of the company. Our recruitment network covers Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan, and Turkmenistan — enabling us to find highly skilled and reliable specialists across Central Asia.

    Our agency is based in Uzbekistan and proudly cooperates with a wide range of entrepreneurs and companies. We handle all stages of the process, including:

    • Verification of candidate qualifications and work history
    • Support with pre-departure training, if required
    • Assistance with visa applications and document preparation
    • Interview coordination — or we can conduct interviews on your behalf
    • Language and cultural adaptation guidance where needed
    • Ongoing communication with candidates before and after deployment

    We value transparency, speed, and efficiency in every collaboration. Whether you need one employee or a team of specialists, Zixan ensures high standards and full compliance with international hiring practices.

    If you're looking for a recruitment partner that understands the regional market and delivers results — we are ready to support you.
