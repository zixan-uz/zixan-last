Workforce Training and Collaboration

At Zixan, we actively collaborate with Uzbekistan's Employment Center (Mehnat) to prepare qualified personnel for international work opportunities. Our agency conducts thorough assessments of each candidate's profession, experience, and reliability. We also support them in acquiring the necessary skills and knowledge required for employment abroad.

Through strong cooperation with government-backed training centers (mono-centers) established in each region of Uzbekistan, candidates receive comprehensive training in technical skills, soft skills, and foreign languages. We guide individuals in adapting to foreign work environments, cultures, and professional standards.

Among the many mono-centers are:

  • Tashkent Mehnat Center
  • Andijan Mehnat Center
  • Samarkand Mehnat Center

Additional national portals we cooperate with include:

  • Xorijda Ish — international labor migration support
  • Mehnat.uz — Uzbekistan’s national employment platform

This collaboration ensures that every Zixan candidate is equipped with practical experience, verified credentials, and readiness to work in line with global standards.
